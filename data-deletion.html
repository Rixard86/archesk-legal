Archesk — User Data Deletion

Last updated: 15 July 2026

This page explains how to delete your Archesk account and the personal data associated with it. It applies to all accounts, including those created with Facebook Login or Google Sign-In.

Request account and data deletion

To have your account and data deleted, send us a request:

  1. Email richard.c.hansson@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Archesk account.
  2. Use the subject line "Delete my Archesk data".
  3. Include the display name or alias on your account so we can locate it. If you signed in with Facebook or Google, tell us which one.

We will verify that the request comes from the account owner and confirm by email once the deletion is complete, normally within 30 days.

What is deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes your profile and the data tied to it, including your profile details, scoring sessions and statistics, equipment, competition entries and ratings, duels, friend connections, shared sessions, messages, and uploaded images.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Some records may persist in encrypted backups for a limited period before they are overwritten, and we may retain the minimum information required to comply with legal obligations.

Removing Archesk's access to your social login

Deleting your Archesk account also ends our access to your Facebook or Google profile. You can additionally remove Archesk directly from your provider:

  1. Facebook: Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites → select Archesk → Remove.
  2. Google: Google Account → Security → Third-party access → select Archesk → Remove access.

Questions

If you have any questions about deleting your data, contact richard.c.hansson@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
